### Thoughts on Second Brains
- Definition
- Personal experience
- Gist: digital systems like Obsidian are [[Projections]] from the Mind into physical space. This *does* aid in thinking systemically afterward.
- One remembers Information by using it. This can be achieved by refactoring that same information by projecting it onto a specific format, for example a markdown document.

Therefore, an RAG (Retrieve Augmented Generation) system on top of obsidian would be counterproductive. This would enable the user to fulfill the formal goal of a note taking system––retrieving info––but would not enforce the rewarding task of embedding the information into the vault, and connecting it to existing notes.
